    • KPIs & Goals

    Goals

    During my tenure at Protocol Labs, the original aim was to create a social media strategy and grow their social audience but after becoming the only marketing member of the team left, my task shifted to create and grow the marketing channels for both the Protocol Labs Research and for their main event, Funding the Commons.

    KPIs

    The KPIs that we set out to acheive the above where:


  • Number of leads for their grant programs.

  • Number of attendees for Funding the Commons event.

  • Increase the revenue generated for Funding the Commons events.

  • Increase community size across social media & number of subscribers to email newsletter.

    • The Plan
  • Create a marketing strategy based on the audience (researchers, tool builders, investors)

  • Educate and entertain our audience on our grant programs and events (Fudning the Commons, Consensus Day, etc.).

  • Increase the volume of posting per day accross the channels to increase visibility of our accounts.

  • Research the key influencers and organizatons in the crypto industry and begin interacting with their posts to expand our reach and potential new audience.

  • Explore new channels that may be beneficial.

    • The Results
  • Skyrocketed the social media audience by a staggering 240% and maintained a remarkable average engagement rate of 3.7% by crafting captivating content, fostering a dynamic community, and executing laser-focused ad campaigns.

  • Boosted event revenue 108.33% through targeted ads, community partnerships, and a winning go-to-market strategy.

  • Catalyzed 375% growth in newsletter subscriptions and 67.5% open rate, 5.4% click-through rate in email marketing through increased frequency, targeted audience segmentation, and compelling content.
