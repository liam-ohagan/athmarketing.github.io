    Goals

    During my tenure at Koinly, the main aim of the game was growth.
    The goal of growth was to grow the social media account sizes and to create an engaging community and that's exactly what we did.

    KPIs

    The KPIs that we set out to acheive the above where:


  • Audience Size (Per Platform & Overall)

  • Engagment Rate

  • Impressions

  • Benchmark vs Competitors (Audience Size, Growth rate, Enagement rate & Post volume)

    • The Plan
  • Prioritize growth over sales.

  • Educate and entertain our audience to make crypto taxes simple to understand and become the thought leader in the space.

  • Increase the volume of posting per day accross the channels to increase visibility of our accounts.

  • Research the key influencers and organizatons in the crypto industry and begin interacting with their posts to expand our reach and potential new audience.

  • Explore new channels that may be beneficial.
